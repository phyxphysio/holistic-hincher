About Holistic Hincher

After recognizing the need for unpolluted, purifying, and wholesome home cleans, and a passion for 'green cleaning', Holistic Hincher was born. Inspired by the famous Mrs. Hinch, the Instagram sensation and British 'cleanfluencer', Holistic Hincher's passion is in keeping things in tip-top condition, transforming homes, and making life easier for their clients. We focus on domestic cleaning using products that not only clean effectively but also disinfect, are non-toxic, and have a great long-lasting fragrance.

We all take pride in maintaining a clean, healthy home, but often the thought of endless cleaning can leave you feeling overwhelmed. At Holistic Hincher, our goal is to make your life easier by handling the cleaning for you. So, whether it's just a routine cleaning package you need, or something as specific as a pantry cleanout and repack, or a quick home reset before your guests arrive for a special dinner, we will tailor a package to suit your unique needs.

We have a team of dedicated cleaners who are handpicked from the best of the best and are devoted to providing the highest level of service and care for your home.
